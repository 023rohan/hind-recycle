OUR MISSION

ABOUT US

Our mission is to keep as much electronic waste from ending up in local landfills as we can

To work with utmost sincerity to dispose off and reduce at least 50 percent of e- waste produced in India sustainably.

We strive to find means to use things again instead of throwing them so that resources can be utilized in best possible manner for a worthy cause.

To ensure that the e-waste produced, from consumers or producers, is disposed sustainably, without negatively affecting the environment, by reaching out to the maximum number of people possible..

How it Works

A Simple Process

Upon scheduling the pick-up of your e-waste, our facilitators will collect and transport the waste to our R2 certified facilities. This reverse logistics process is managed by our own fleet of vehicles and facilitated through our channel partners. In doing so, we can directly ensure the safe transport of both hazardous and non-hazardous e-waste to our facility.
